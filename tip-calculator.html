How much should you tip?

Tipping norms vary by country and service type. In the US, 18–20% is now the standard for restaurant servers, with 15% considered the minimum for acceptable service and 25%+ for exceptional service. Tip percentages should generally be calculated on the pre-tax total.

Is tip calculated before or after tax?

Tip on the pre-tax subtotal — that's the standard. Some restaurants display suggested tip amounts on receipts that are calculated on the post-tax total, which inflates the tip by a few percent. This calculator uses the bill amount you enter, so just enter the pre-tax amount if you want to be precise.
