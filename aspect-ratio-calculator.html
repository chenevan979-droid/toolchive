About This Calculator

An aspect ratio describes the proportional relationship between width and height. It's expressed as W:H (e.g. 16:9) or as a decimal (1.778).

Common aspect ratios

  • 16:9 — Widescreen HD/4K TV, YouTube, most monitors
  • 4:3 — Older TVs, tablets, many cameras
  • 1:1 — Square (Instagram, profile photos)
  • 21:9 — Ultra-wide monitors, cinema
  • 3:2 — DSLR cameras, 35mm film

Frequently asked questions

How does this aspect ratio calculator work?

The aspect ratio calculator simplifies a width and height to their lowest ratio (for example 1920×1080 becomes 16:9) and can also scale dimensions to a new size while keeping the same proportions.

How do I calculate an aspect ratio?

Divide the width and height by their greatest common divisor. For example, 1920 and 1080 share a divisor of 120, which gives 16:9. The tool does this for any dimensions.

How do I resize an image without distortion?

Use the scale mode: enter your original size and one new dimension, and the tool calculates the other dimension to preserve the exact aspect ratio.
